Crossfit is often joked as being a cult. To me it is a community of people who come together to grow and learn together. Let me tell you why I love crossfit so much.

  1. Community
    • Friends are hard to come by as a full time worker, husband and father. I get to see my Crossfit buddies almost every day.
    • Being surrounded by others encourages me to push harder than if I were to exercise alone.
    • Being around athletes more skilled than myself creates an environment that encourages growth.
  2. Diversity of exercises
    • Olympic lifting
    • Gymnastics
    • Cardiovascular training
    • Traditional weightlifting
  3. Coaching
    • Direct feedback on how to improve
    • Optional one on one coaching is often available
    • Performance improves at a much faster rate than if athletes were to train alone
